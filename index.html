Common Questions

FAQ

Do I own the website?
Yes, 100%. The domain is registered in your name and I can hand you the files at any time. You're never locked in with me.
How long does it take?
Most sites go live within 5–7 days of our first call. Premium package gets priority treatment at 3 days.
What do you need from me?
Your logo (if you have one), any photos of your work, list of services, and your contact info. I handle everything else — most clients send this in a text thread.
Will people find me on Google?
Yes. Every site includes local SEO setup and Google Search Console connection so Google indexes it within days of going live.
Can you update it if my info changes?
On the maintenance plan, just text or email me and I handle it within 48 hours. No tech knowledge needed on your end, ever.
I already have a website — can you fix it?
Absolutely. Rebuilds are common and keep your existing domain so you don't lose any Google history. Same flat-fee pricing applies.
